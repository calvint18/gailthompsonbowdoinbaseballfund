Gail's Story
Gail is a special person in the Thompson family and her full story can not be captured with just words. To encapsulate the authentic emotion and personality, the Thompson family has volunteered to make a video discussing Gail's story and their experience taking care of an individual with Alzheimer’s. Please give it a watch to understand the full picture of why support for Alzheimer’s is necessary.
